Welcome to Grace Farm Site!

Grace Farm is a livestock farm located in Kampala, Uganda

It started in 2001 and has had quite a number of achievements during its existance.

It was started by one person who is still the owner and works along with fifteen other members.

It is a private owned entity that focuses on derivering best and high agricultural products to the clients


The farm mainly deals in rearing chicken, goats, cattle and sheep.

It also supplies animal feeds, sells manure, animal products like milk, eggs and meat.

Additionally, the farm also provides quidance to farmers who are raring different livestock and quidance to those who want to start up farming.

This guidance includes; tips on how to start up a farm e.g the size of the housing, ventilation, etc.

To the people who are already doing farming, they can consult the veterinary doctors of the farm and get help.

The farm also sells animall vaccines and feeds, it also provides vaccination services and artificial insemination.

About Us

Grace Kisitu opened up a farm in 2001 and called it Grace Farm. It was started with a goal of becoming the number one farm in Uganda that provides best agricultual products and services.

Grace Farm products include; Chicken eggs, chicken, manure, cows, goats and milk.

It also provides paid for services like; Vaccination, artificial insemination.
